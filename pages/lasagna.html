Lasagna Recipe


Ingredients

  • 1 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 medium red onion, finely diced
  • 2 large cloves minced garlic
  • 8 ounces pancetta, diced
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups good red win, preferably Italian
  • 2 28-ounce cans Italian plum tomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons tomato paste

Steps:

  1. Heat up the sauce and add your onions, minced garlic, and pancetta.
  2. Combine the sirloin, cheese and eggs in a large bowl.
  3. Heat the remaining oil in a large skilelt over medium-high heat.
  4. In a clean skillet, brown the sausages over medium-high heat.
  5. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Combine your ricotta, eggs, pecorino romano, parsley and mozzarerlla.
  6. Eat you pig.
