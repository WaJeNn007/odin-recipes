Spaghetti


Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can tomatoe sauce
  • 1 can tomatoe paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Steps:

  1. Combine beef, onion, garlic, and pepper in large sauce pan.
  2. Cook and stir until meat is brown and vegetables are tender.
  3. Drain Grease
  4. Stir diced tomatoes, tomatoe sauce, and tomatoe paste into the pan.
  5. Season with oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  6. Simmer spaghetti sauce for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
